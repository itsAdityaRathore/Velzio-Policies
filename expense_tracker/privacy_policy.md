## Overview

Expense Tracker is designed with privacy in mind. Your financial data is stored locally on your device. We do not have our own servers and do not store your personal financial information. When you use AI features (optional), aggregated spending summaries are sent to Google Gemini for processing — see the "Google Gemini AI" section below for details.

---

## Data We Store on Your Device

All of the following data is stored **locally on your device** in an SQLite database. It is never uploaded to any server.

- **Transactions:** Amount, date, category, and optional notes you enter
- **Categories:** Custom category names, colors, and icons you create
- **Smart categorization hints:** Keywords the app learns from your usage to suggest categories
- **Settings:** Theme preference (light/dark/system), currency, and month start day

No account creation or sign-in is required. No bank linking or cloud sync is used.

---

## Camera Access

The app includes a receipt scanner feature that uses your device camera to extract text from receipts. All text recognition is performed **on-device** using Google ML Kit. Photos taken or selected are processed locally and are **never uploaded or transmitted** to any server.

Camera access is only used when you explicitly open the receipt scanner.

## Third-Party Services

### Google AdMob

We display ads through Google AdMob to support free access to the app. AdMob may collect certain device and usage data as described in [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). This may include:

- Device identifiers (advertising ID)
- IP address
- General location (derived from IP)
- App usage data related to ad interactions

You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings:
**Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization**

### Google Play Billing

The app offers in-app purchases and subscriptions through Google Play Billing:
- **Ad-Free** — one-time purchase to remove all ads
- **Pro** — monthly or yearly subscription for AI insights, unlimited chat, and receipt scanning

Purchase and subscription transactions are handled entirely by Google Play. We do not collect or store any payment information. Subscriptions auto-renew until cancelled. You can cancel anytime from Google Play > Subscriptions.

### Google Gemini AI (Pro / AI Insights)

The app's AI features (weekly reports, overspend alerts, chat) use **Google Gemini API** to generate insights. When you use these features, the following data is sent to Google's servers for processing:

- **Aggregated spending summaries** (category totals, weekly/monthly averages) — NOT individual transaction details
- **Your question text** (for AI chat)
- **Currency and language preferences**

This data is processed by Google under their [Gemini API Terms of Service](https://ai.google.dev/gemini-api/terms). Google may use this data as described in their privacy policy. We do not store any data on our own servers.

**Important:** AI-generated insights are for informational purposes only and do not constitute financial, investment, or legal advice.

### Firebase Crashlytics

We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect anonymous crash reports to improve app stability. This may include device model, OS version, and stack traces. No personal or financial data is included in crash reports.

## Data We Do NOT Collect

- Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number)
- Financial account information (bank accounts, card numbers)
- Location data (GPS)
- Contacts, call logs, or messages
- Individual transaction details sent to external servers (only aggregated summaries for AI features)

---

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any of your data with third parties. The only third-party data collection is by Google AdMob as described above, which can be eliminated by purchasing the "Remove Ads" upgrade.

---

## Data Retention and Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device. You have full control:

- **Delete individual transactions** within the app
- **Clear all data** by clearing the app's storage in your device settings
- **Uninstalling the app** permanently deletes all associated data

We retain no data on any server.
