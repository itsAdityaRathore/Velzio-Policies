Privacy Policy

Expense Tracker by Velzio

Last updated: April 18, 2026

Overview

Expense Tracker ("the App") is built by Velzio. This policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your choices.

Data We Collect

Data stored on your device only:

  • Transactions and categories you create
  • App preferences (theme, currency, display settings)

This data is stored locally in an on-device database and is never uploaded to our servers. We do not have access to your financial data.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may collect data independently:

  • Google AdMob — serves ads and may collect device identifiers, IP address, and ad interaction data. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play Billing — processes in-app purchases (e.g., "Remove Ads"). Purchase data is handled by Google. See Google Payments Privacy Notice.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging — delivers push notifications (reminders, spending insights, announcements). Firebase may collect device tokens and message delivery metadata. No financial data is sent.
  • Firebase Remote Config — allows us to update app configuration remotely (e.g., feature flags, display text). No personal data is collected.
  • Firebase Crashlytics — collects anonymous crash reports including device model, OS version, and stack traces to help us fix bugs. No financial or personal data is included in crash reports.
  • Google ML Kit — powers on-device receipt scanning (OCR). All text recognition happens locally on your device. No images or extracted text are uploaded.

Permissions

  • Internet — required to load ads, process in-app purchases, receive push notifications, and send crash reports.
  • Camera — used for receipt scanning, if you choose to use that feature. Images are processed on-device and are not uploaded.
  • Notifications — used to send daily expense reminders, spending insights, and announcements. You control which notifications you receive via in-app settings. All notification types are off by default.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. The only data leaving your device is what AdMob and Google Play Billing collect as described above.

Data Retention & Deletion

All app data is stored locally. You can delete all your data at any time via Settings → Clear all transactions. Uninstalling the App or clearing its data from your device settings permanently deletes all your data. We retain nothing on our end.

Under GDPR and similar regulations, you have the right to request deletion of your data. Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control: clear your data from the app settings, or uninstall the app to remove everything. No request to us is needed.

Data Processing

The App uses Firebase services provided by Google. Data processing by Firebase is governed by Google's Data Processing Terms. Firebase processes anonymous crash data, notification delivery metadata, and remote configuration requests. No financial data or personally identifiable information is included.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
